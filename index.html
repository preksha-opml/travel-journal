🚀 Get your blog online — free

  1. Go to github.com and create a free account
  2. Click + New repository → name it travel-journal → tick "Add README"
  3. Click Add file → Upload files → upload this travel-journal.html file
  4. Rename the uploaded file to index.html (click the file → pencil icon)
  5. Go to Settings → Pages → Branch: main → Save
  6. Your blog is live at yourname.github.io/travel-journal 🎉
⚠ Photos you upload are stored in your browser only — they won't appear when someone visits your GitHub Pages link. To share photos publicly, upload them to Google Photos or Imgur, copy the image link, and paste it into the photo URL field (coming in next version). For now, use the journal for writing + social captions.

📱 Sharing tips

  • Instagram: Use the caption generator per entry. Post your photos + caption. Put your blog link in bio.
  • WhatsApp: Hit "WhatsApp" on any entry — it opens a pre-filled message you can send to any group or contact in one tap.
  • Family/friends: Share your GitHub Pages link directly — it works on any phone or computer, no app needed.
